SceneDetails and LocationPortals rules:

If a LocationPortal points to a scene, that target scene must be reachable via SceneDetails.connectedScenes from the source scene at runtime.

However:

This rule does not apply to world↔world adjacency

It does apply to any portal-based transition, including:

World → Interior

Interior → World

Interior → Interior (if you ever add that)
