### ✅ Quick Notes on Each Column:

* **Detection Radius:** How close the player must be to trigger reaction.
* **View Angle:** Cone of vision (0–360°). Small for timid animals, wide for predators.
* **Omnidirectional?:** Ignores facing, can see in all directions.
* **Reaction Delay:** Time before starting flee or chase; works with the “!” alert.
* **Max Chase Dist:** Leash distances; territorial Pokémon often have spawn=0, territory>0.
* **Give Up Time:** How long without progress before stopping chase/flee.
* **Safe Distance:** Target distance from threat to stop fleeing.
* **Max Flee Dist:** Maximum distance Pokémon will flee before exhaustion.
* **Flee Exhaustion:** Cooldown before Pokémon can flee again.
* **Flee Accel:** Time to ramp from slow start to full flee speed.
* **Chase Move Delay:** Base delay per step; affects overall movement speed.
